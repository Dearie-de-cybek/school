About Us

Building Skills That Shape The Future

Ofagbe Technical College is dedicated to producing skilled artisans, technicians, and innovators through practical, hands-on education. Since our establishment, we have continued to provide technical and vocational training that meets industry standards and prepares students for successful careers.

Practical Training

Every student gains real-world experience in fully equipped workshops designed for hands-on learning and technical mastery.

Community & Industry Focus

We work closely with local industries and communities to ensure our programs remain relevant and impactful for today’s job market.
