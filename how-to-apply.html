How To Apply

Simple Steps To Join Ofagbe Ogbe Tech

Applying to Ofagbe Ogbe Tech is straightforward and hassle-free. We've designed our admission process to be as simple as possible so you can focus on what matters most - learning your chosen trade. No complex procedures, no unnecessary delays. Just bring your documents, choose your course, and you're ready to start.

Our admissions office is open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Our friendly staff will guide you through every step of the process and answer all your questions about courses, fees, and schedules.

Things To Know First

Before applying to Ofagbe Ogbe Tech, it's important to understand what makes us different. We focus on practical, hands-on training that prepares you for real-world work. Our courses are intensive and require commitment, but the rewards are worth it. You'll be working with actual tools and equipment from day one, not just theory in a classroom.

Classes are conducted in small groups to ensure personalized attention from instructors. Attendance is mandatory as missing practical sessions can affect your learning progress. We also maintain strict safety standards in our workshops, so you'll learn proper safety procedures alongside your technical skills. Upon completion, you'll receive a certificate that's recognized by employers across Nigeria.

Required Documents & Payment

To complete your registration at Ofagbe Ogbe Tech, please bring the following documents to our admissions office. All documents should have both originals and photocopies.

  • Birth certificate or age declaration from local government
  • Educational certificates (Primary school certificate or WAEC/NECO for some courses)
  • Four recent passport-sized photographs with white background
  • Valid means of identification (National ID card, voter's card, or driver's license)
  • Guarantor's information (to be completed during registration)

Payment Options: We accept full payment, two-installment plans, or monthly payments. Payment can be made via cash, bank transfer, or POS at our office.

Contact Admissions Office

Applying is simple! Visit our campus during working hours (Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM) with your documents. Meet with our admissions officer, choose your course, complete the application form, and make payment. There's no entrance exam required.

You'll need your birth certificate or age declaration, educational certificates (primary or secondary school), four passport photographs, valid ID card (National ID, voter's card, or driver's license), and guarantor information. Bring both originals and photocopies of all documents.

We offer flexible payment plans to make education affordable. You can pay in full and receive a 10% discount, pay in two installments, or arrange monthly payments. We accept cash, bank transfer, and POS payments at our office.

We have multiple intake periods throughout the year. Most courses start at the beginning of each month. Once you complete registration and payment, you'll be informed of the next available start date for your chosen course. Contact our admissions office for specific dates.
