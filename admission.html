Admission Guide

Start Your Journey To Professional Excellence

Welcome to Ofagbe Ogbe Tech! We make the admission process simple and straightforward. Our doors are open to anyone ready to learn a valuable technical skill. Whether you're a school leaver, career changer, or looking to upgrade your skills, we're here to help you succeed.

We offer flexible admission throughout the year with multiple start dates for different courses. Our affordable fees and payment plans ensure that quality technical education is accessible to everyone.

  • Simple and quick admission process

  • Flexible payment options available

  • Multiple intake periods per year

  • No entrance examination required

Requirements

Admission Requirements

Our admission requirements are straightforward and designed to be inclusive. We believe everyone deserves the opportunity to learn a valuable skill.

Educational Qualification

Minimum of primary school certificate or equivalent. Some courses may require secondary school certificate (WAEC/NECO/GCE).

Age Requirement

Applicants must be at least 16 years old. There is no upper age limit - learning is for everyone at any stage of life.

Physical Fitness

Good health and physical fitness for hands-on practical work. Some courses require ability to lift tools and materials.

Process

Simple Application Steps

Follow these easy steps to secure your admission at Ofagbe Ogbe Tech and start your journey toward a rewarding technical career.

01

Visit Our Campus

Come to our campus to see our facilities and speak with our admissions office. Bring all required documents.

02

Choose Your Course

Select the technical course you want to study. Our staff will guide you to make the best choice for your goals.

03

Complete Application

Fill out the application form and submit it along with your documents and passport photographs.

04

Pay Fees & Start

Make payment (full or installment) and receive your admission letter. Classes begin as scheduled.

Documents

Required Documents

Please bring the following documents when you come for registration:

  • Birth certificate or age declaration

  • Educational certificates (Primary/Secondary school)

  • Four recent passport photographs

  • Valid means of identification (National ID, Voter's Card, etc.)

  • Guarantor's form (will be provided at registration)

Note: All documents should have both original and photocopies.
